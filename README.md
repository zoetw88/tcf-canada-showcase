# TCF Canada

**An AI speaking coach for French immigration exam preparation.**

TCF Canada helps learners practice French speaking with a clear improvement loop: choose a target level, answer realistic prompts, receive AI-guided feedback, and return to focused review.

[Launch Web App](https://tcf-canada-gamma.vercel.app)

![TCF Canada product preview](assets/desktop-preview.png)

## The Problem

French speaking preparation is hard to do alone. Learners often know they need more practice, but they do not always know what to fix after each answer.

TCF Canada is built around that gap: it gives learners a structured way to practice speaking and understand the next improvement to make.

## Product Positioning

TCF Canada is not a generic language app. It is a focused AI practice product for learners preparing for French-speaking immigration exams.

The product helps users:

- Build a repeatable speaking practice habit.
- Work toward target levels such as A1, A2, B1, and B2.
- Receive structured feedback after practice.
- Save useful prompts for later review.
- Track progress and return to weak areas.

## AI Product Experience

### AI Speaking Feedback

Each practice attempt becomes a feedback moment. The product highlights how an answer can become clearer, more fluent, and better organized.

### Level-Aware Guidance

Learners choose a target level, and the feedback experience helps them understand the gap between their current answer and the level they are working toward.

### Personalized Review Loop

Users can save practice items and return to them later, turning one-time practice into a focused review workflow.

### Practical Improvement Notes

Feedback is designed to be understandable and action-oriented, so users know what to try in their next answer.

## Privacy & Safety

TCF Canada is designed with privacy, safety, and exam-prep clarity in mind:

- Feedback is learning guidance, not an official exam score.
- Public materials do not include private user data.
- Account controls include export and deletion readiness.
- Source code, credentials, model prompts, internal operations, and release artifacts are not published.
- Monitoring is used for reliability without exposing private operational details.

## Product Principles

- Make AI feedback specific and useful.
- Keep the practice loop simple and fast.
- Design for learners who study independently.
- Prioritize speaking improvement over visual noise.
- Be clear that practice feedback is not an official exam result.
