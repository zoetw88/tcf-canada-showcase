AI feedback and topic memory

Practice one answer, then review a focused improvement target.

SpeakCanada AI is designed for learners who practice alone but still need clear feedback. After an answer, the app can generate feedback, connect the attempt to similar prompt groups, and keep saved examples available for future topics.

  • Past-exam inspired speaking prompts without claiming official affiliation
  • Interactive AI feedback and next-practice prompts after a French answer

  • Saved personal examples that support related question recommendations

Practice, review, repeat

The experience stays focused on the learner's next useful action: pick a categorized speaking question, answer in French, save a reusable example, and return to related prompts that practice the same theme from another angle.
