AI interaction and topic memory

Practice one answer, then continue with focused follow-up questions.

SpeakCanada AI is designed for learners who practice alone but still need interaction. After an answer, the app can generate follow-up questions, connect the attempt to similar prompt groups, and keep saved examples available for future topics.
